For Article Submissions

The Curious Citizen welcomes guest submissions for publication.

Please contact us with your submission according to the below guidelines at the following e-mail address: submissions@curiouscitizen.org

We will respond to your submission within one calendar week.

Guidelines for News

E-mail subject line: News Article Submission

Submissions should be between 250 and 1000 words in length. Submissions that do not meet this requirement will still be considered for publication. Submissions longer than 1000 words may be considered for publication in our Journal.

Please attach your submission in PDF, DOCX, or TXT format.

In your e-mail, please submit two suggested titles and two suggested subtitles for your article.

Guidelines for Journal

E-mail subject line: Journal Article Submission

Submissions should be between 1000 and 5000 words in length. Submissions that do not meet this requirement will still be considered for publication. Submissions shorter than 1000 words may be considered for publication in The Curious Citizen News.

Please attach your submission in PDF, DOCX, or TXT format.

In your e-mail, please submit two suggested titles and two suggested subtitles for your article.
