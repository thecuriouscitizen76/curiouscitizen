About The Curious Citizen

Mission of The Curious Citizen

The Curious Citizen seeks to advance the timeless ideals of liberty, security, and truth by sharing research, data, and analysis from a variety of perspectives.

Why The Curious Citizen Was Established

In an 1810 address to the United States Congress, President James Madison once reminded our nation that "a well-instructed people alone can be permanently a free people." With this attitude in the forefront of our minds, The Curious Citizen seeks to promote American liberty, security, and truth through curiosity and excellence in scholarship.

What You Can Find on This Website

The Curious Citizen is a collection of articles containing independently conducted research and new data supporting fresh approaches to previously or currently established ideas.

At the The Curious Citizen, we seek to provide to our readers thought-provoking work to strengthen scholarship and understanding in America and across the world.
